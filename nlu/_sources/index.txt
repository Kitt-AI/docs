Welcome to KITT.AI NLU
====================================

`KITT.AI NLU`_ is an interactive and intuitive Natural Language Understanding
engine, providing "understanding as a service". It is supposed to be used
with KITT.AI's ChatFlow_ (docs_), providing "conversation engine as a service".
Together they fulfill KITT.AI's mission: "Conversational Understanding as a Service".
